Example Instructions and Tutorial
----------------------------------

1. Open 3D Print UnFucker.exe

2. Click "Open Gcode" button and navigate to /Program Files/3D Print Unfucker/example/dock.gcode

3. Input the line number at which your 3D print failed

4. Input the height as defined by your slicer or gCode file

5. Input the desired Start print temperature

6. Click "UNFUCK GCODE" button

7. Select "Yes" to open gcode in slicer of choice, click "No" to complete

8. Reveiw your file and compare to 'example/Print Recovery Template.txt'
